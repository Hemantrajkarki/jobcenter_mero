Since its inception in 2009, Merojob has been at the forefront of connecting job seekers and employers in Nepal. The goal is to provide a comprehensive platform for job seekers to find jobs in Nepal and for employers to find the right fit for their organization. We pride ourselves on being a reliable bridge between hiring employers and job seekers and have established ourselves as a national leader in recruitment solutions.
